Cook Restaurant was founded in the 90’s by an engineering student in his final year. He couldn’t taste home-cooked food, so he created a restaurant to provide people with the food they long for when they miss home cooking. This is truly a unique dining spot where you can indulge in delicious home-style cooking even when you're away from home. it's the perfect place to savor comforting dishes that remind you of homemade meals. the cozy ambiance and mouthwatering flavors make it a standout choice for those seeking a taste of home outside of town.
